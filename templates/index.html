About TalkP

Empowering Your Persian Learning

TalkP is an AI-powered platform that helps learners of Persian (Dari) master the language through smart tools and personalized training.

Your Personal Persian Language Assistant

From vocabulary building to pronunciation and grammar correction — everything you need to boost your Persian skills in one place.

  • Vocab Trainer: Add words to your DariBrain, hear pronunciation, and see grammar-based usage.
  • Language Level Test: Find out your current Persian proficiency and track your progress.
  • Grammar Correction: Write better with real-time feedback on Persian grammar.
  • Text-to-Speech: Listen to native-like pronunciation to improve your speaking skills.

TalkP uses state-of-the-art Persian NLP models to provide a smooth and effective learning experience for learners of all levels.
